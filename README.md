# EL NIŇO s.r.o. — Construction Company Website

## Company Overview

**EL NIŇO s.r.o.** is a stable and reliable construction company based in **Skalica, Slovakia**. They operate across the **Skalica and Senica districts**, offering a wide range of construction services — from new builds and full renovations to smaller repairs and ongoing maintenance.

Their tagline (translated from Slovak):
> "We are a stable and reliable construction company operating in the Skalica and Senica districts."

They position themselves as a full-service construction partner: whether you're building your dream home from the ground up, renovating an apartment, or need emergency plumbing repairs, EL NIŇO handles it. They emphasize **modern and professional design with attention to detail**.

**Free price quotations and on-site inspections are provided as standard** — no cost or obligation.

EL NIŇO also offers decorative brick cladding to give homes a rustic countryside charm — using either new or reclaimed bricks. Their brick cladding process includes:

### Specialty: Foundation Construction (Základové dosky)

They offer **complete foundation slab construction** — from initial surveying with a geodesist all the way through to final concreting. This is pitched to customers building their dream home who need solid, correctly designed and executed foundations.
